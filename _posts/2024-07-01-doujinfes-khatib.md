Doujinfes Khatib turned out to be the last Doujinfes I would table at for the foreseeable future as I will be going on booth hiatus after Cosfest 2024. This event was also the last time I went maskless. As I kept getting sick this year, I decided to wear masks at events and see if it will make a difference.

As I had events every weekend of June, I took it easy for Doujinfes Khatib. I did not create any new merchandise for the event nor did any online marketing. When Doujinfes Tampines Safra first launched in September 2023, I was part of the artist alley there. After that, I also tabled at Doujinfes Bedok before landing at Doujinfes Khatib. I‚Äôm happy that more people are noticing and attending this event. Doujinfes is one of my favorite events to table at.

Doujinfes events are free entry and very spacious. There is always enough space to chill and relax with friends. As an exhibitor I especially enjoy the 1.8m tables that events hardly provide anymore. The booth lots are spacious and there is free water distributed throughout the map. This event also allows setting up on Fridays. I absolutely prefer this as it will be less hectic for me. Kudos to the Doujinfes team for proving such a wonderful tabling experience consistently!
